How A Stubborn Counter Culture Finally Flipped The Narrative


made with a thick heart and a slim attitude

by Zhao Yang - Dec 2021

Slim Thick is a female body type that consists of a flat stomach, toned thighs, and large booties originally celebrated as a counter culture over 5 decades ago when Playboy founder Hugh Hefner popularized the "36-24-36" ideal female body measurement. The original voluptuous body form has since out lived the 70s Androgyny and 90s Heroine Chic, and eventually was transformed and popularized by the Kardashian family.

See how the counter culture dodged decades of peddulum swings and mutated into the ideal body type of our time.

36-24-36
