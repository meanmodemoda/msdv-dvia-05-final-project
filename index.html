This is a visual data story of my own probe into the rise in popularity of the Slim Thick body type in America. As a resident alien, I have come to observe that American women and particularly teen girls’ beauty aspirations have becoming increasingly homogeneous while the country itself is coming apart. Be it Instagram or TikTok, if you search for #fitness, the results often pull you into a rabbit hole of videos of young girls working out for a Slim Thick body.

Slim Thick is an uncommon female body type that consists of a flat stomach, toned thighs, and large booties, originated from a voluptuous body form celebrated as a counter culture over 5 decades ago. It was eventually transformed and popularized by the Kardashian family in conjunction with the rise and dominance of social media. Let's go back briefly in time and see how the counter culture dodged decades of pendulum swings and mutated into the ideal body type of our time.
