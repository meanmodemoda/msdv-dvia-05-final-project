by Zhao Yang - Dec 2021

Slim Thick is an uncommon female body type that consists of a flat stomach, toned thighs, and large booties, originated from a voluptuous body form celebrated as a counter culture over 5 decades ago when Playboy founder Hugh Hefner declared the "36-24-36" ideal female body measurement. The alternative body type has since out lived the 70s Androgyny and 90s Heroine Chic, and was eventually transformed and popularized by the Kardashian family in conjunction with the rise and dominance of social media.

Let's go back briefly in time through photos and lyrics and see how the counter culture dodged decades of peddulum swings and mutated into the ideal body type of our time.
